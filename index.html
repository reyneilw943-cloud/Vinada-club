Message from the President

Dear friends and members of Vinada,

It is my pleasure and honour to welcome you to the VINADA CLUB, home to the vibrant cultures of St. Vincent and the Grenadines and Grenada. As President, Iam excited to share with you who we are, what we represent, and the values that unite us.The VINADA Club was founded with a simple yet meaningful purpose: to create a space where students from our two beautiful islands can celebrate their identity, support one another, and proudly showcase our rich heritage.

Join us as we continue building a culture of excellence and inclusivity.

— Vayani Toney, President
